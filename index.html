About This Project

What problem does this solve?

Imagine you run a business. A thousand people visit your website. But only 30 of them actually buy something. Where did the other 970 go? At what step did they leave? And most importantly, why?

That is exactly what a funnel analysis answers.

I took raw customer journey data, analysed every stage of the funnel- from awareness all the way to conversion, and turned it into a clear visual story. No jargon. No complex spreadsheets. Just clean, honest numbers that show what is working and what is not.

How I Did It

Step by step — here is my process

  • Collected and cleaned the data - Organised raw data into a structured, analysis-ready format
  • Identified all funnel stages - Mapped the full user journey from first touch to final conversion
  • Calculated drop-off rates - Found exactly where and how many users were leaving at each step
  • Built the Looker Studio dashboard - Designed an interactive report that anyone can explore in real time
  • Extracted business insights - Turned the numbers into clear, actionable recommendations
What I Found

What the data revealed

  • The biggest drop-off happens early - Most users leave in the first 1–2 stages, long before they ever reach the purchase step
  • A small fix can have a big impact - Even a 10% improvement at the highest drop-off stage can significantly increase final conversions
  • Not all traffic is equal - Some sources bring visitors who convert well. Others bring volume but almost zero results
  • The data tells you exactly where to invest - Instead of guessing, a business can now point to a specific stage and fix it with confidence
